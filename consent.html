II Contraindications Confirmation

I confirm that I am currently free from any of the following conditions which would render me unsuitable for this treatment:

  1. Active facial infection, acute dermatitis, unhealed wounds, ulcers, or currently experiencing a severe acne breakout.
  2. Skin conditions such as eczema, psoriasis, rosacea, or fungal dermatitis.
  3. Pregnancy, lactation, or active attempts to conceive.
  4. Known allergies to any product components that may be used during the treatment (e.g., specific essential oils, cleansing agents).
  5. History of intense facial sun exposure within the past week, or currently exhibiting post-sun exposure reactions such as redness or a burning sensation.
  6. Excessively thin stratum corneum coupled with current symptoms of sensitivity including redness, stinging, or peeling.
  7. (Specific to Dermaplaning) Use of depilatory creams in the treatment area within the past week, or undergoing laser/IPL hair removal or waxing within the past 1–2 weeks.
  8. Currently undergoing chemotherapy or radiotherapy.
  9. Epilepsy or history of seizures.
III Post-Treatment Care Compliance

1. General Post-Treatment Requirements: For 24 hours post-treatment, avoid activities that induce heavy sweating such as saunas or hot spring baths. Increase daily hydration. Use only gentle skincare products and avoid those containing alcohol, acids, or abrasive particles. Avoid exfoliation for one week. Strict daily application of a broad-spectrum sunscreen with SPF 50+ is required.

2. Specific Instructions:

  • Aromatherapy Meridian Therapy: Mild discomfort or tenderness post-treatment is a normal physiological reaction to the meridian stimulation. It typically subsides within 24–48 hours.
  • Deep Pore Cleansing (Acne-Prone Skin): Pay attention to dietary habits post-treatment, reducing intake of high-sugar and high-fat foods. Strictly avoid direct sun exposure for two weeks.
  • Dermaplaning: If skin redness occurs, a cold compress may be applied for relief.
IV Acknowledgment of Potential Risks

I acknowledge and understand that receiving this treatment involves the following potential risks, despite the studio taking professional measures to minimize their likelihood:

  1. Temporary skin redness, mild swelling, irritation, tightness, or short-term flaking may occur post-treatment.
  2. In rare instances, hyperpigmentation or hypopigmentation may occur.
  3. Improper post-treatment care (e.g., picking, failure to maintain cleanliness) carries a risk of infection or scarring.
