IV Aftercare Guidelines 术后护理指引
  1. Avoid sauna, strenuous exercise and excessive sweating within 24 hours after treatment.
  2. Do not exfoliate within one week; refrain from using acidic products, exfoliating scrubs, alcohol-containing and high-concentration Retinoid irritant products.
  3. Apply broad-spectrum sunscreen daily (SPF30 or above recommended, SPF50+ preferable) due to strong UV radiation in New Zealand; avoid prolonged sun exposure.
  4. Temporary redness, tightness and dryness after treatment are normal skin reactions, which generally subside within 1–3 days.
  1. 护理后24小时内避免桑拿、剧烈运动及大量出汗。
  2. 一周内勿去角质；避免使用酸性产品、去角质磨砂膏、含酒精及高浓度维A酸类刺激性产品。
  3. 每日涂抹广谱防晒霜（建议SPF30以上，SPF50+更佳），新西兰紫外线辐射强烈，避免长时间日晒。
  4. 护理后出现暂时性发红、紧绷及干燥属正常皮肤反应，通常1-3天内自行消退。
