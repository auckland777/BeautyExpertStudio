Our Device

Q Facial
Water Shining System

3-in-1 Skin Management System with Ultrasonic

Q-facial is a comprehensive skin management device using three types of management systems. It sprays three formula solutions onto the skin for absorption and management, removing dead skin cells, sebum, and blackheads to accelerate skin turnover. A high-concentration skin booster soothes and improves skin that lacks elasticity. The ultrasonic system increases hydration to improve the skin's regenerative ability and maintain elastic skin.

How It Works

3-Step Mechanism

01

Spray

The solution is sprayed, turning clockwise through the centre of the nozzle tip, dissolving impurities on the skin surface.

02

Vacuum

Skin impurities and solution residue are vacuumed into the waste bottle through the mouth of the nozzle.

03

Deliver

The solution or booster ampoule is delivered to the skin surface according to the setting for deep hydration and soothing.

Professional Solutions

Three Formula Solutions

AHA

Formula-A: Glycolic Acid (AHA)

Water-soluble exfoliation formula. Helps with overall skin cell turnover and cleanses the pores, softening impurities inside pores.

Recommended: Normal / Dry / Sensitive Skin

BHA

Formula-B: Salicylic Acid (BHA)

Oil-soluble exfoliation formula. Helps to remove the sebum and cleanse the pores for residue inside pores.

Recommended: Oily / Problem Skin

TON

Formula-C: Hydrating & Soothing (TON)

Strong hydrating and soothing finishing formula. Contains hyaluronic acid (sodium hyaluronate) and vitamin E (tocopheryl acetate) to balance skin hydration and oil.
