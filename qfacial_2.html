How It Works

The 3-Step Mechanism

Spray, vacuum, deliver — a closed-loop care cycle in one handpiece.

01

Spray

The solution sprays in a clockwise spiral through the centre of the nozzle, dissolving impurities on the skin surface.

02

Vacuum

Impurities and solution residue are vacuumed into the waste bottle through the mouth of the nozzle.

03

Deliver

A solution or BELLELUMIE booster ampoule is delivered to the skin for deep hydration and soothing.
